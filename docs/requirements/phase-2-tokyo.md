### Sunday antique-market interest (day-23)

User interest: "public small auctions about old ancient legacies." Traditional dealer-syndicate auctions (e.g. 東京美術倶楽部) are trade-only. Public paths = open-preview auction houses + public antique markets.

- **大江戸骨董市 (Oedo Antique Market) — PRIMARY** — **Tokyo International Forum open plaza, 千代田区丸の内3-5-1.** ~250 dealers (Japan's largest outdoor). **1st + 3rd Sundays, 09:00–16:00, free.** **day-23 = 2027-01-17, 3rd Sun → confirmed in-window.** Coverage: 着物/帯, 浮世絵, 陶磁 (伊万里/九谷/有田), Meiji/Taishō glass, 仏像, 根付, 刀剣, 古銭. ⚠️ Outdoor — day-before update at antique-market.jp; rain/snow may cancel.
- **Public-preview auction houses (if schedules align — check ~1 mo out):**
  - **毎日オークション** (my-auction.co.jp/schedule/index) — 2–3 day preview before each sale, walk-in.
  - **Shinwa Auction (シンワオークション)** — 2-3-2 Marunouchi. 3-day preview.
  - **東京中央オークション (Tokyo Chuo Auction)** — heavyweight for Chinese calligraphy / painting / porcelain if user's interest tilts Chinese ancient art.
  - **SBI Art Auction / 廻 MEGURU / Tokyo Culture Auction / iArt / A.C.N. Auction Tokyo** — smaller houses, "small auctions" vibe.
- **Out-of-window (noted so user doesn't plan around it):** 平和島全国古民具骨董まつり — Dec 5–6 and Mar 2027 editions flank our window.
